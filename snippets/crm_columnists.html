Jancis Robinson

WINE COLUMNIST

Jancis Robinson is an award-winning wine critic, journalist and editor of wine literature who writes a weekly column for FT Weekend. A qualified Master of Wine, She travels globally to conduct wine events and act as a wine judge, and in 2003 was awarded an OBE by Queen Elizabeth II, whose cellar she now advises.
